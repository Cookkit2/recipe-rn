## 2026-03-28 - [Added comprehensive accessibility props to Auth Input and Social Buttons]\n**Learning:** React Native custom interactive components (like a `Pressable` acting as a button or toggle) require not just an `accessibilityLabel`, but an explicit `accessibilityRole="button"` and an `accessibilityState={{ disabled }}` for screen readers to fully understand their function and state. Implicit labels (like a `Text` child inside a `Pressable`) do not guarantee the disabled state or role is accurately conveyed.\n**Action:** Whenever creating or modifying a generic `Pressable` that functions as a button, form control, or interactive toggle, always ensure all three props are applied: `accessibilityRole`, `accessibilityLabel` (or dynamically changing label based on state), and `accessibilityState` if there's a disabled or loading variant.
