## 2026-03-16 - Added accessibility labels to recipe images\n**Learning:** Image components used in essential visual areas (like Recipe Stack or Item Cards) often lack `accessibilityLabel` properties, rendering them invisible to screen readers. Relying solely on adjacent text may lose context (e.g., when the image is the primary interactive touch target or visually represents the item).\n**Action:** Always verify that `<Image>` components rendering content-heavy media include a contextual `accessibilityLabel` derived from the content's title or description, especially when rendered in lists or carousels.
